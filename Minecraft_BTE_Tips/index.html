Tips for ASEAN BTE Builders

Worldedit

You all know of the traditional way of building right? Well, we didnt use that. Instead, we implemented a plugin named Worldedit. Worldedit is a plugin that can place blocks and replace them at will. Its basically your bread and butter!


Map of the world.

You can also find other players on the world by pressing 'M'. By doing so, it pulls up a geographical map of the world and shows players location. If you are someone with special access, you can teleport to them!


Amenu

A special thing we implemented is Amenu! Its a menu where you can warp/teleport to places and monuements!
