ASEAN Home

Welcome to Team ASEAN! Team ASEAN is a unified team which consists of countries that are from the South East Asia region. We will oversee the progression of Thailand, Malaysia, Singapore, Vietnam, Philippines, Brunei, Myanmar, Cambodia, Indonesia, Laos and Timor-Leste throughout the building process. What is ASEAN? ASEAN is an organization called Assosiation of Southeast Asian Nation that gathers all South East Asian countries to make a unified system for the good of all participating countries. Like the ones in real life, we are aiming to do the same with Build The Earth..

How to install BTE Modpack.

STEP 1

Install The ZIP from the BTE Website. Click on the link to download!

STEP 2

Unzip the modpack. You should get a folder from your ZIP.

STEP 3

You should get a Windows SmartScreen notification. It should say 'Windows protected your PC. Click on more info and run the installer. Do not worry. This is a normal process.

STEP 4

Now, you should have an installer window. You can click on optional mods to enable things like Terramap, WorldEdit, and other mods like VanillaFix or ReplayMod.

FINISH

After doing that, click on install or if you have the launcher already, click update. After that, you are already on your way to build the earth in Minecraft!
